Demographic and Health Surveys (MEASURE DHS)

Getting Started

To start, select a series and a country from the left and click Show Series. Wait a few seconds for the interactive graph and map to render. You can mouse over and click districts on the map to view additional details.

District means, seasonality-adjusted means (trend), and standard deviations may be downloaded in CSV or STATA formats. The 1960-2013 rasters for each country may also be downloaded in GeoTiff, ASCII Raster, and netCDF formats for use in a desktop GIS.
