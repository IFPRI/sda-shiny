About

Over 200 unified indicators on mother and child health for 60 countries around the world are available for display and download.

To get started, simply select an indicator and a country on the right and click Show Indicator. Wait an instant for the interactive graphs and map to render. You can mouse over and click regions on the map to view additional details. Each sub-national indicator may be downloaded in CSV, STATA, or ESRI Shapefile formats. All statistics are derived from four rounds of MEASURE Demographic and Health Surveys spanning 15 years from 1999 through 2014.
